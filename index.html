What Is This Website For?

Hello! Welcome to Wolverine Math! This is a collection of resources curated by Emily _ and Nico _ designed to aid students in their high school math classes. This site is being built by hand, and is best viewed on a laptop or desktop computer. No mobile optiomization, sorry!


Who Are We?

We are seniors in high school (class of 2023) who run our high school's math tutoring club. We have been close friends for many years, and the idea for this project came to us the summer after our junior year.
